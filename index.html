Scaling Ethereum with Layer 2

Discover how Arbitrum solves Ethereum's scalability challenges while inheriting its unmatched security. The future of decentralized finance is fast, cheap, and secure.

🐌

Why Layer 2?

Ethereum Mainnet (Layer 1) is incredibly secure and decentralized, but it can only process ~15 transactions per second. This causes network congestion and exorbitant gas fees during peak times. Layer 2 solutions build on top of Ethereum to handle computation off-chain.

⚡

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is an Optimistic Rollup. It executes transactions on its own cheaper network, bundles them together (rolls them up), and posts the compressed data back to Ethereum Mainnet. This dramatically reduces the cost per transaction while maintaining L1 security.

💸

Real-World Benefit

Using Arbitrum over Mainnet means you can swap tokens, buy NFTs, or interact with DeFi protocols for pennies instead of tens of dollars. It makes Web3 accessible to everyday users without compromising on the decentralized ethos of Ethereum.
